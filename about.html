About Us

What is MIMOS Academy?

Established in association with the Malaysian government, MIMOS Academy aims to drive the development of high-tech talent across the nation. Our comprehensive learning programs focus on semiconductor, cybersecurity, and AI to power the next generation of technology leaders.

Our students gain direct access to cutting-edge labs, world-class mentors, and a growing network of opportunities in the global technology and semiconductor industries.

Our Purpose

We build real-world technology experts at the intersection of innovation, research, and industry readiness — empowering talent to drive Malaysia's digital transformation.

Our Mission

To empower the next generation of technology experts by delivering industry-relevant, hands-on training that bridges the gap between academic knowledge and professional competency in Malaysia's key technology areas.

Our Vision

To become Malaysia's premier technology academy recognized globally for producing world-class talent in semiconductor, AI, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies that power the future of innovation.
